# Daily Discovery: Castelo de Torres Novas
**Country:** Portugal

The Castle of Torres Novas (Portuguese: Castelo de Torres Novas) is a medieval castle in the civil parish of Torres Novas (São Pedro), Lapas e Ribeira Branca, municipality of Torres Novas, Portuguese district of Santarém.It is classified as a National Monument.
